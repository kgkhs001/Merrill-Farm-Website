The Barn Restaurant


Here at the Barn at Merrill Farm Inn, we wish to offer our guest and friends the relaxing and casual atmosphere of a rustic barn combined with the freshest ingredients that only local farms can provide. We work with a number of farms in our area that provide us with seasonal foods, delivered weekly. This gives us the chance to mix our offerings regularly to suit the focus of each of our farm partners. Autumn/Winter 2020-2021 Dinner will be open most Fridays and Saturdays. Please check with the hotel regarding the dates you are staying with us.
