Cabins

When you visit the White Mountains what could be better to stay in a cabin. With their charming rustic feel, our cabins are a wonderful way to get away from it all and still have the luxuries you want to enjoy your vacation. Each cabin is located along the Saco River where you can scroll to great views of the White Mountains all year long. They’re all quite unique, varying in layout and decor. We have three types of cabins here at Merrill Farm; Couple’s Cabins, Family Cabins and One Bedroom Cabins. Couple’s cabins include one queen sized bed, mini-refrigerator, microwave, cable TV, and full bathroom. Family cabins include all that couple’s cabins do, plus a kitchenette and pull out sofa bed. One Bedroom cabins are Family cabins with a private bedroom.

All cabins have fireplaces (electric) and are pet friendly, have shared grills, river access, and parking right out front.
