Our Loft Rooms

Our Loft Rooms are very large suites with more living space than hotel rooms. The loft suites have 2 separate floors with the stairwell in the suite. The first floor has cathedral ceilings against the outside wall that is overlooked by the second floor bedroom area. Every loft room has a mini-refrigerator, cable television and a DVD player. All lofts have large 37 inch televisions on each floor.

Our regular loft rooms each contains two separate sleeping areas. The loft rooms can sleep up to 6 people and have two separate vanity areas. These suites have the classic feel of an old fashion ski lodge.

Our large loft suites have two separate bathrooms; one on each floor. The large loft suites can sleep from 6 to 8 people comfortably. The lofts all have a 37 inch flat screen cable television.

Our King Lofts have 1 king bed, sofa bed and 1 queen bed in the loft area. The lofts all have a 37 inch flat screen cable television. These suites also have the classic feel of an old fashion ski lodge. The King Loft can sleep up to 6 people.
