Our Inn Rooms

Nothing beats the romantic New Hampshire Bed and Breakfast. Our Inn rooms are what make us the quintessential New England Inn. Each Inn Room is located in the original farm house dating back to the 1800s. The modernization of each room gives our guests the luxuries they deserve at a price they can afford. We have taken the time and care to decorate each Inn Room in fine details and charm. Our Inn Rooms range in décor from the Bear Room to the Blue Room. All of our Inn Rooms are so unique and stylish we have named each one after classic authors from years gone by.

Every Inn Room has its own private bathroom as well as a mini-refrigerator and a 37″ flat screen cable television . The modern appliances are not in the bathroom that would be too crowded.
