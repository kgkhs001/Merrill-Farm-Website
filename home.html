A True New England Inn

The Merrill Farm Inn & Resort is a wonderful New England inn nestled among beautiful trees and lovely landscaping on seven full acres. Its rustic and warm, as well as tranquil country charm will make you feel at home with us. We are known for our warm hospitality and quality service.

Hotel Rooms

Our Hotel rooms are designed to keep the look & charm of the rustic inn with the added amenities of the modern age

Lofts

Our lofts are truly one of a kind accommodation. Large enough to accommodate your entire family, from 6 to 8 people, in one large room with a barn loft. Our lofts have one full bath and large lofts have two full baths

Cabins

Our standalone cabins are great for couples or families. The cabin experience is true to its New England origins. Most of our cabins overlook the Saco River and have kitchenettes
